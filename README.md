## 💡 Overview

StrategyPilot Elite is an AI-powered system that helps companies understand:

- who they compete with  
- where they are weak  
- what they should build next  

It transforms raw company context into **actionable strategic insights**.

---

## 🎯 Why this exists

Large enterprises have:
- strategy teams  
- consultants  
- market intelligence tools  

But early-stage companies don’t.

They rely on:
- intuition  
- fragmented research  
- slow manual analysis  

👉 StrategyPilot Elite bridges this gap using AI.

---

## ⚙️ What it does

Given:
- company name  
- sector  
- business context  

The system generates:

- 🔍 Competitor discovery (semantic similarity)  
- 📊 Capability analysis  
- ⚠️ Gap detection  
- 💡 Strategic recommendations  
- 📄 Executive-style summary  

---

## 🧠 Core Components

### 1. Capability Extraction Engine
Identifies key business capabilities from company context  

### 2. Competitor Matching Engine
Uses semantic similarity to find relevant competitors  

### 3. Gap Analysis System
Compares company vs competitors across capability dimensions  

### 4. Strategy Engine
Generates actionable business and product recommendations  

### 5. Executive Brief Generator
Produces structured output for decision-making
